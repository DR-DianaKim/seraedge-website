OUR MISSION

Building the Future of Data Infrastructure

SERA Inc. stands for Sustainable Environment Renewable Architecture. We are revolutionizing the data center industry through innovative passive and active air-hybrid cooling technologies that dramatically reduce energy consumption while maximizing performance.

Our mission is to create next-generation edge AI modular data centers that are not only highly efficient but also environmentally responsible. With our proprietary BREED BOX technology, we're setting new standards for sustainability in critical digital infrastructure.
